Our zoo occupies almost 750 acres and is home to more than 3,000 animals representing over

A Few Words About Bonobomb Zoo

Our zoo is the largest and oldest zoological garden established in 1920. The animals
located at Bonobomb Zoo are grouped according to their geographic territories, with
species that live together peacefully in the wild placed in exhibits together here at the
zoo.
